Autism Spectrum Disorder


Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects an individual’s communication abilities, social interactions, and behavioral patterns. Individuals diagnosed with ASD often face difficulties in interpreting social cues, expressing emotions effectively, and adapting to changes in routine. Early detection and timely intervention play a crucial role in improving developmental outcomes and enhancing the quality of life. This system aims to support early screening by leveraging advanced deep learning techniques to assist in accurate and efficient ASD identification.
