Introducing the System

About ASD

Welcome to the ASD Detection System, a state-of-the-art AI-driven platform developed to facilitate early and accurate detection of Autism Spectrum Disorder. Leveraging three advanced Deep Learning models, the system analyzes input data to deliver precise predictions for children aged 2 to 18 years. By integrating cutting-edge technology with rigorous real-world data validation, the platform ensures reliability and clinical relevance. The intuitive interface allows healthcare professionals and parents to access actionable insights seamlessly. The system is designed to support early intervention programs and personalized care plans, enabling timely decisions that can significantly impact developmental outcomes. It incorporates advanced feature extraction techniques to analyze behavioral patterns, facial expressions, and other relevant indicators. Continuous model training ensures adaptability and improved accuracy as more data is collected over time. The platform also provides comprehensive reports that can be used for follow-ups and consultations with specialists. With strict adherence to data privacy and security standards, the ASD Detection System empowers families and professionals with trustworthy, evidence-based insights, promoting informed decision-making and better healthcare outcomes.
